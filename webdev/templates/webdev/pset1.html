Day 1: HTML

Problem Set #1: First Website

For this problem set, create a website about anything you'd like (can be yourself, pet, favorite book or even food). Make sure the website met following requirements:

  • At least 2 headings
  • At least 3 paragraphs
  • At least 1 ordered list
  • At least 1 unordered list
  • At least 2 images
  • At least 1 hyperlink
  • At least 1 bold text
  • At least 1 italic text
  • At least 1 underlined text
